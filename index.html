TheDooo

TheDooo is an American musician who plays video games on YouTube. He is from Missouri and his real name is Eric. He usually plays his guitar in his videos but has been known to sing and feature some of his piano skills on the channel. He has over 1.3 million subscribers and has over 130 million views. He was originally known as MountinDooo, but then changed it to TheDooo, and no one knows why.

TheDooo first started playing guitar when he was around 13 years old. His first instrument was the violin, but he ultimately got tired of the instrument and started playing the guitar his dad had lying around the house. His dad had taught him the basics, but he is mostly self-taught. He started singing when he first started to write music, so he wanted to put vocals to it and decided to give it try.
